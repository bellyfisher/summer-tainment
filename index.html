Band Camp 2025!

What is Marching Band?

Band Camp is a long process, usually spanning across two weeks, around six hours a day.
During these hours, we spend time looking over drill sheets and sheet music for our field show.
Band camp is a big commitment.
You MUST be in the class if you are joining marching band, save for the exception of students in the business program.
